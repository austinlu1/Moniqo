News & Updates

Moniqo is in Taiwan

Moniqo has expanded its reach internationally, bringing financial literacy and career preparation programs to students in Taiwan. This milestone marks a major step in our mission to make real financial education accessible worldwide.

Partnership with Bridge Finance Network

Moniqo has partnered with Bridge Finance to bring real-world financial experience to NYC students. Through this collaboration, students gain hands-on exposure to investing, budgeting, and financial decision-making.

Moniqo will be bringing financial literacy tutoring sessions to 353K in Brooklyn, expanding our reach and impact within the local community.
